About Me

I am Mallvin and I am passionate about technology, technological advancements and trying to improve the world with technology everyday with my entrepreneurial passion too.

I am a third year Singapore Polytechnic student specialising in Software Development. I have experience creating both node js and Java J2EE full stack web applications.

I have experience in AR, VR and mix reality working with unity and C# building application such as training simulators.

Furthermore, I have worked with image recognition technologies like OpenCV and machine learning libraries like TensorflowTensorflow GPU enabled and to create various image recognition models based on business needs such as illegal parking detection and people counting. I also have experience working woth various Anaconda libraries and tools.

I have learnt to also do programming for data science where I clean datasets and process them using analytical libraries like numpy, pandas, matplotlib and seaborn with jupyter notebook to generate useful business insights.

Moreover, I have also experienced working with IOT devices such as Arduino boards to create magnetic gates that can be controlled via API to open or close the gate through web application or mobile application after valid processing.
